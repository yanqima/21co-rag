Sustainable Energy Solutions

The transition to renewable energy is critical for combating climate change. Solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal power are leading the way in creating a sustainable energy future.

Renewable Energy Types:
1. Solar Power - Photovoltaic cells convert sunlight to electricity
2. Wind Energy - Turbines harness wind to generate power
3. Hydroelectric - Using water flow to produce electricity
4. Geothermal - Tapping Earth's internal heat
5. Biomass - Converting organic materials to energy

Benefits:
- Reduced greenhouse gas emissions
- Energy independence
- Job creation in green sectors
- Lower long-term costs
- Improved public health

The global renewable energy capacity has been growing exponentially, with costs decreasing rapidly.
